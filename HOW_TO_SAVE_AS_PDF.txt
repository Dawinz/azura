To get a PDF of "Deployment & Corrections Documentation":

1. Open this file in your browser:
   DEPLOYMENT_AND_CORRECTIONS.html

2. Press Ctrl+P (Windows) or Cmd+P (Mac) to open the Print dialog.

3. Set the destination to "Save as PDF" or "Microsoft Print to PDF"
   (exact name depends on your OS/browser).

4. Click Save and choose where to save DEPLOYMENT_AND_CORRECTIONS.pdf.

The HTML file is print-optimized (the blue instruction box is hidden when printing).
